Playbooks

Turn education into action.

These stay compact until clicked, so the page feels like a product hub instead of a textbook.

Free

Prepare to Sell

Clean the story before buyers pick it apart.

  1. Update rent roll and T12.
  2. Explain expense spikes before launch.
  3. Check violations, taxes, leases, and legal use.
  4. Build a realistic value range with upside clearly separated.
Growth

Refinance

Know whether the income supports the debt.

  1. Calculate NOI from actuals.
  2. Test DSCR, debt yield, LTV, and rate sensitivity.
  3. Review upcoming CapEx and reserve needs.
  4. Compare refinance proceeds against sale value and hold strategy.
Growth

Renovate / Improve

Spend only where it moves value.

  1. Identify rent, expense, or condition bottlenecks.
  2. Estimate cost, downtime, and achievable rent lift.
  3. Prioritize improvements that raise NOI or reduce risk.
  4. Avoid vanity upgrades that buyers will not pay for.
Free

Hold / Optimize

Improve operations before deciding to sell.

  1. Benchmark taxes, water, heat, insurance, and repairs.
  2. Track rent vs. market.
  3. Fix operational leaks before they reduce value.
  4. Revisit valuation after NOI improves.
Growth

Buy / Underwrite

Separate real upside from broker math.

  1. Start with actual income, not pro forma.
  2. Normalize expenses realistically.
  3. Model debt and exit cap sensitivity.
  4. Require proof for every upside claim.
Free

OM Prep

Make the asset easier to understand and trust.

  1. Prepare property facts, unit mix, photos, and maps.
  2. Separate current income from upside.
  3. Highlight zoning, tax, and regulatory details.
  4. Use clean, defensible numbers.
