Broker FAQ

Common Questions

Does BRIC compete with brokers or replace them?+
Neither. BRIC makes brokers better. Owners come to us for clarity — then many engage brokers when they're ready to transact. Our Professional Access Layer connects owners to brokers at the moment of highest buying intent. For subscribing brokers, that means warmer leads, better deals, and shorter cycles.
Is my data and client information private?+
Absolutely. Your workspace, listings, client rosters, and communications are private to your account or team. We never share broker data with owners, other brokers, or third parties. Team accounts have granular permission controls.
How does the Owner Intent Radar actually work?+
As owners use BRIC to analyze their property — running valuations, exploring comps, building OMs — we generate anonymized signals by submarket and property type. Subscribing brokers see these signals mapped to their territory, with property-level detail available for qualified matches. All data is aggregated and privacy-compliant.
Can I white-label OMs with my brokerage logo?+
Yes. Broker Solo includes basic white-label (logo on cover). Team and Enterprise plans include full white-label with custom colors, typography, and brokerage-specific templates. Enterprise customers can fully remove BRIC branding.
What markets do you currently cover?+
BRIC launched with NYC and NJ coverage. We're expanding to LA, Chicago, Boston, and Miami in 2025. Enterprise customers in other markets can contact us for early-access partnerships.
Can I integrate BRIC with my CRM?+
Team and Enterprise plans include integrations for Salesforce, HubSpot, and Follow Up Boss. Enterprise customers get API access for custom integrations. Individual brokers can export contacts and listings as CSV.
